Domestic Purchases

Contact us directly to place an order from the US or Canada.

International Purchases

Contact a dealer in your region to place an order.

When sending a Purchase Order (PO), please be sure to include:

  1. A complete and accurate list of Part IDs
    Check the StoneAge Sales Portal for accuracy and availability. If time frame is critical, please contact our Customer Service Team to verify availability.
  2. Shipping address
  3. Required date of arrival
  4. Preferred shipping method and account number
  5. If partial shipments are acceptable or a complete shipment is required

Shipping

StoneAge can ship with the following carriers:

  • UPS
  • FedEx
  • DHL
  • UPS/SCS
  • Multiple freight carriers —please check availability

Please provide a preferred shipping method and account number, if applicable. Shipping charges can be included on your invoice, or we can ship on an account number you specify.

Verify your order

Your StoneAge Account Representative will always send an order confirmation. Please check the confirmation and notify our Customer Service Team if you notice any mistakes or have any questions/concerns.

Repairs

When sending equipment in for repair, please be sure to:

  1. Obtain a Return Material Authorization (RMA) from your Account Representative
  2. Provide Part ID(s) — if sending a package, please include all part numbers. (i.e. BN18-H6L, BN18 442-D)
  3. Provide Serial Number(s)
  4. Provide the pressure and flow the tool is operating
  5. Describe the problem in detail

The tool will be evaluated by a repair technician upon arrival and you will be contacted by StoneAge with a repair estimate. A PO is required to proceed with the repair.

Returns

When sending a tool back for a return, please be sure to:

  1. Obtain a RMA from your Account Representative
  2. Provide Part ID(s)
  3. Provide Serial Number(s)
  4. Describe the reason for return (i.e. Demo tool, wrong part ordered, wrong part sent, return for credit, etc.)

When the tool arrives it will be evaluated for condition. Your Account Representative will communicate with you regarding the return.
Returns are subject to a 15% restock fee.
