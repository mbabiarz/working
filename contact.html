Repairs

When sending equipment in for repair, please be sure to:

  1. Obtain a Return Material Authorization (RMA) from your Account Representative
  2. Provide Part ID(s) â€” if sending a package, please include all part numbers. (i.e. BN18-H6L, BN18 442-D)
  3. Provide Serial Number(s)
  4. Provide the pressure and flow the tool is operating
  5. Describe the problem in detail

The tool will be evaluated by a repair technician upon arrival and you will be contacted by StoneAge with a repair estimate. A PO is required to proceed with the repair.

Returns

When sending a tool back for a return, please be sure to:

  1. Obtain a RMA from your Account Representative
  2. Provide Part ID(s)
  3. Provide Serial Number(s)
  4. Describe the reason for return (i.e. Demo tool, wrong part ordered, wrong part sent, return for credit, etc.)

When the tool arrives it will be evaluated for condition. Your Account Representative will communicate with you regarding the return.
Returns are subject to a 15% restock fee.
