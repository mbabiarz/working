Sabertooth

Share
  • E-mail link

The StoneAge Sabertooth SRT-100 is an automated flex lancing system specifically engineered to clean large tube bundles such as those found in industrial condensers and evaporators. The Sabertooth is the first flex lance system in the industry that comes standard with hose containment and an auto-stroke feature to maximize safety and increase efficiency.

Features

  • Hoses are contained and automatically managed in an integrated reel
  • Auto-stroke function reacts when hitting blockages and adjusts to keep nozzles spinning
  • High/Low speed forward drive maximizes cleaning efficiency in various conditions
  • Dual lance system cleans two tubes at once to increase productivity
  • Easily adapts to horizontal or vertical tube cleaning
  • Shotgun compatible for cleaning the bundle face and tube IDs with a single setup
  • Can be disassembled with a standard wrench to fit through manways as small as 24 in. (0.6 m)
