Boiler Blaster

Share
  • E-mail link

The Boiler Blaster is an efficient and highly adaptable modular system for cleaning the exteriors of tubes and fins found in boiler economizers and air pre-heater exchangers. The Boiler Blaster uses high pressure and flow, an adjustable head angle, and controlled traverse rates to produce exceptional and consistent cleaning performance.

Features

  • Frame is modular and can be customized to fit any enclosure
  • Requires only a single operator to run; freeing crew members for other jobs
  • Use higher flows for better jet penetration
  • Adjustable jet rotation and traverse speeds for optimal dwell time
  • Optional tilting head for maximum jet penetration between tubes
  • Portable frame allows for easy relocation
