Cyclone Backwall Cleaner

Share
  • E-mail link

The StoneAge Cyclone Backwall Cleaner is designed specifically for cleaning internal chambers of a cyclone furnace typically found at coal fired power plants. The unit is unique in the industry for itâ€™s ability to fold compactly for insertion and then open up inside the cyclone furnace for cleaning which eliminates the need for operator entry.

Features

  • Unique design expands to meet the inner diameter of the cyclone back wall
  • No need for variances or confined space entry permits
  • Controlled remotely by a single operator
  • Use higher flows for better jet penetration and refractory removal
  • Adjustable jet rotation and traverse speeds for uniform cleaning
