AutoBox ABX-2L

  • Share

The StoneAge AutoBox ABX-2L is an automated system for hands-free heat exchanger tube cleaning applications. It was designed with an emphasis on safety, ease of setup, portability and reliability.

Features

  • Lightweight, modular design for single operator use
  • Accommodates hose sizes from 3/2 to 6/4
  • Adjustable pitch for many tube sizes
  • Independent forward/reverse speed control
  • High quality components withstand corrosive environments
  • Remotely adjustable hose clamp pressure allows operator to adapt to changing conditions and minimize hose wear
  • Reliable anti-withdrawl protection
  • Dual side access doors and quick-swap rollers simplify maintenance
  • Compact control box features ergonomic, low effort control levers and pneumatic dump switch
